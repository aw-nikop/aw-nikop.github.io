Alton Computer Solutions

Alton Computer Solutions was established in 2006 with the idea of exceptional service and support paired with robust software and hardware solutions. Our goal is to help your business succeed by providing experienced computer and networking support in a timely, professional manner with proven software, hardware, and Cloud Solutions.

Alton Computer Solutions has more than two decades of experience creating hardware and software solutions for hundreds of small businesses. Our staff enjoys working closely with customers to create systems that adapt to their unique business needs.

Alton Computer Solutions is the outsourced IT provider for Seattle-area businesses. We are up to speed on the latest IT Solutions that work reliability and will not break the bank. We listen to our customers and do not force a “one size fits all” solution. Instead, we build a computer network around your specific business needs.
